Carpentry is a skilled trade in which the primary work performed is the cutting, shaping and installation of building materials
during the construction of buildings, ships, timber bridges, concrete formwork, etc.
Carpenters traditionally worked with natural wood which has been prepared by splitting (riving), hewning, or sawing with a
pit saw or sawmill called lumber (American English) or timber (British English).
